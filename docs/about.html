About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Katherine Kiessling, a rising senior at the University of Michigan pursuing a dual degree in Computer Science from the College of Engineering and Dance from the School of Music, Theatre & Dance. Hailing from the beautiful city of Traverse City, Michigan, I have developed a passion for both the logical world of programming and the creative realm of dance.

With a strong foundation in C++, Python, and Matlab, I possess a solid understanding of programming languages and their application in various domains. Additionally, I am well-versed in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Arduino, Vue, and SQL, enabling me to develop dynamic web applications and explore the exciting possibilities of the internet.

My frequent utilization of GitHub and Visual Studio Code allows me to manage and collaborate on projects efficiently. Whether working on programming challenges or choreographing dance routines, I approach every endeavor with dedication, attention to detail, and a strong desire to grow as a professional and artist continuously.

Furthermore, throughout my journey as a dancer, I have had the privilege of training and performing across the United States and Europe with esteemed companies such as Company Dance Traverse, Vim Vigor, b12, and Sidra Bell, among others. These experiences have not only honed my technical skills and artistic expression but have also enriched my cultural understanding and appreciation for diverse dance styles.

In addition to performing, I have a strong passion for teaching. Sharing my knowledge and love for dance brings me great joy, and I strive to inspire and empower others through my teaching. I believe in the transformative power of dance education and its ability to impact lives positively.
